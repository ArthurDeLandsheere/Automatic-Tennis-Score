# Player Detection & Filtering Evaluation

This document details the evaluation results for the **tennis player detection** module. It assesses the performance of a pre-trained YOLOv8m model detecting the COCO `person` class, and measures the impact of a custom single-frame spatial filter designed to isolate the two main players.

## 1. Dataset & Methodology

* **Dataset:** Evaluated on the [tennistracker-dogbm](https://universe.roboflow.com/tennistracker-dogbm/tennis-tracker-duufq) dataset from Roboflow Universe.
* **Evaluation Metric:** A predicted bounding box is considered a **True Positive (TP)** if its Intersection over Union (IoU) with a ground truth player bounding box is strictly greater than **0.50**. 
* **Ground Truth Filtering:** The dataset annotations were filtered to exclude the "ball" class so that only human players were evaluated.

The evaluation was performed in two stages to measure the impact of spatial filtering:
1.  **Without Filter (Raw YOLOv8):** Evaluates all raw `person` detections generated by YOLOv8m across the frame.
2.  **With Filter (Spatial Logic):** Evaluates the detections with the filter considering the court

---

## 2. Quantitative Results

Applying the spatial filter drastically reduces the number of False Positives (ghost detections/crowd members), nearly doubling the overall F1-Score, though it comes at the cost of some Recall. 

| Metric | Raw YOLO (No Filter) | After Filter | Delta |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **True Positives (TP)** | 1,051 | 799 | *- 252* |
| **False Positives (FP)** | 3,221 | 231 | *- 2,990* |
| **False Negatives (FN)** | 27 | 279 | *+ 252* |
| **Precision** | 24.60% | **77.60%** | *+ 53.00%* |
| **Recall** | **97.50%** | 74.10% | *- 23.40%* |
| **F1-Score** | 39.30% | **75.80%** | *+ 36.50%* |
